COWABUNGA, BABY

It's feeding time.

Have you ever wanted to feed a cow? Of course you have. But here's the problem: the cow is hungry. And you've dropped the food. What ever will you do?

Collect Food

Go around the farm and collect the food items to put in the cow's feeding bowl. Different foods will make him have different powers and make him fatter! The fatter he gets, the slower he gets.

Avoid the cow

You don't want to touch the cow. Why? He's hungry. He may mistake you as a carrot and try to eat you.

Play with your friends.

Race side by side with your friends to see who can feed the cow the most food before time runs out!
